1. Introduction

BeePORT Technology ("Company", "we", "us", "our") operates the BeeZee mobile application and related services ("Platform", "App", "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal information when you use BeeZee.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable Indian data protection laws, including the Information Technology Act, 2000, the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011, and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 ("DPDPA") as applicable.

BeePORT Technology acts as the Data Fiduciary in respect of personal data processed through the BeeZee Platform. Personal data is processed only for lawful purposes and in accordance with applicable consent requirements under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. By using BeeZee, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Information You Provide Directly

For Candidates:

  • Full name
  • Mobile number (used as primary identifier)
  • Age and date of birth
  • Gender
  • Trade/skill information (e.g. electrician, plumber, painter)
  • Work experience and education details
  • Location (city, suburb, address)
  • Job preferences (daily/monthly, shift preferences, travel distance)
  • Government job eligibility details (reservation category, education level) — if voluntarily provided
  • Profile photograph — if voluntarily provided
  • Government-issued identity documents (such as Aadhaar, PAN, Driving Licence, Voter ID or other identification documents) voluntarily submitted for verification purposes. Aadhaar information, if submitted, is processed only for identity verification purposes and is not used for profiling or marketing activities.
  • Ratings and feedback about Companies
  • Referral information (names and phone numbers of people you refer)

For Companies:

  • Contact person name and mobile number
  • Business/company name
  • Legal business name, GSTIN — if voluntarily provided
  • Industry and business type
  • Business location (city, suburb, address)
  • KYC documents (PAN, Aadhaar, Udyam certificate) — for verification
  • Job posting details
  • Brand association information
  • Ratings and feedback about Candidates
  • Payment transaction details

2.2. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, app version
  • Location data: GPS coordinates when you grant location permission (used for job matching and nearby Site detection)
  • Usage data: Pages viewed, features used, time spent, search queries, application actions
  • Log data: IP address, access times, app crashes and errors
  • Network information: Mobile carrier, connection type

2.3. Information from Third Parties

  • Payment processors: Transaction status and payment confirmation (we do not store full payment instrument details)
  • SMS/WhatsApp service providers: Delivery status of messages sent to you
  • KYC verification services: Verification results (verified/not verified)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

3.1. Core Platform Services

  • Verifying your identity through OTP and KYC processes
  • Creating and maintaining your user profile
  • Matching Candidates with relevant job opportunities based on skills, location, and preferences
  • Enabling Companies to search for and connect with suitable Candidates
  • Facilitating job applications, hiring, and engagement processes
  • Displaying relevant job feeds and search results
  • Processing subscription payments and Site commission payments

3.2. Trust and Safety

  • Calculating Trust Scores based on verification, ratings, and completion history
  • Detecting and preventing fraud, fake accounts, and platform abuse
  • Reviewing job postings for compliance (pay rate verification, keyword screening)
  • Investigating reports of non-payment, unsafe conditions, or harassment
  • Enforcing our Terms and Conditions

3.3. Communication

  • Sending OTP for login verification
  • Sending job notifications via App, WhatsApp, and SMS
  • Sending hire confirmations, rating prompts, and engagement updates
  • Sending subscription and payment confirmations
  • Sending referral status updates
  • Responding to your support requests

3.4. Improvement and Analytics

  • Understanding how users interact with BeeZee to improve features
  • Analysing job market trends and matching efficiency
  • Monitoring platform performance and fixing technical issues
  • Developing new features and services

3.5. Legal and Regulatory

  • Complying with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
  • Responding to lawful requests from government authorities
  • Protecting the rights, safety, and property of BeePORT Technology and its users

3.6. Information Processing

We process personal information based on user consent, performance of our contractual obligations, compliance with legal requirements, protection of legitimate interests relating to platform security and fraud prevention, and other lawful grounds permitted under applicable law.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1. With Other Users

  • Candidate profiles are visible to Companies that have an active subscription. Profile visibility depends on the Company's subscription tier.
  • Company information (business name, industry, location, trust score) is visible to Candidates when viewing job postings.
  • Ratings and trust scores are visible to other users as part of the trust framework.
  • Site Lead contact details are NOT shared with Candidates or Companies for safety reasons.
  • Personal contact numbers are shared only after a mutual hire confirmation (Candidate accepts, Company confirms).

4.2. With Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating the Platform:

  • SMS and WhatsApp providers (MSG91, 2Factor, Gupshup): Your mobile number and message content for notifications
  • Payment processors (Razorpay): Payment and transaction details for processing payments
  • Cloud infrastructure (DigitalOcean): Data storage and computing
  • Analytics providers: Anonymised usage data for platform improvement

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

4.3. With Government and Law Enforcement

We may disclose your information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request, including:

  • Court orders or subpoenas
  • Requests from law enforcement agencies
  • Tax authorities (for business/payment related information)
  • Labour regulatory bodies

4.4. Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

4.5. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.
  • We do NOT share your data with advertisers.
  • We do NOT use your data for purposes unrelated to BeeZee's services.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1. Storage

  • Your data is stored on secure servers managed by DigitalOcean, located in India (Bangalore region).
  • Database backups are encrypted and stored securely.

5.2. Security Measures

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Hashed and salted passwords for admin accounts
  • JWT-based authentication with token expiration
  • Role-based access control for internal teams
  • Database access restricted to authorised personnel only
  • Regular security reviews

5.3. Limitations

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

6. Data Retention

6.1. Active accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward.

6.2. Deleted accounts: Upon account deletion, we will delete or anonymise your personal data within 90 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as resolving disputes, enforcing agreements, or complying with legal obligations). Users may request deletion through the App settings or by contacting support@beezee.co.in. Upon verification of the request, personal information will be deleted or anonymised within 90 days unless retention is required by law.

6.3. Payment records: Financial transaction records are retained for a minimum of 8 years as required under Indian tax and financial regulations.

6.4. Ratings and trust data: Anonymised ratings data may be retained indefinitely for platform integrity.

6.5. Site operation records: Deployment and commission records are retained for a minimum of 3 years.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection laws, you have the following rights:

7.1. Right to Access

You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

7.2. Right to Correction

You can update or correct your profile information through the App at any time. For information that cannot be self-edited, contact our support team.

7.3. Right to Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data, subject to legal retention requirements. You can delete your account directly in the app (Profile → Delete account) or follow the steps at beezee.co.in/delete-account.html.

7.4. Right to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw consent for specific data processing activities. Note that withdrawing consent for essential services (such as location access) may limit your ability to use certain features.

7.5. Right to Grievance Redressal

You have the right to raise grievances about our data practices with our Grievance Officer (see Contact section).

7.6. How to Exercise Your Rights

Contact us via the methods listed in the Contact section. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

8. Location Data

8.1. BeeZee requests access to your device's GPS location to:

  • Match you with nearby job opportunities
  • Calculate distance between Candidates and job locations
  • Verify proximity to BeeZee Sites (for Site operations)
  • Auto-detect your city and suburb during registration

8.2. Location permission is optional. You can deny or revoke location access at any time through your device settings. Without location access, job matching may be less accurate, and you will need to enter your location manually.

8.3. We do not continuously track your location. Location is accessed only when you actively use the App or when performing specific actions (such as checking in at a Site).
