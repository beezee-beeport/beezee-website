Why We Built BeeZee

India has 450 million blue-collar workers. The people who build homes, wire factories, service appliances, paint walls.

For most of them, finding the next job still means standing at a naka at 6 AM, hoping a contractor picks them up. Technology hasn't reached them.

BeeZee is built ground-up for this audience — voice-first, WhatsApp-enabled, trust-based. Because everyone deserves dignified access to work.
